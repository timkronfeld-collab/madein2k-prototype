madein2k — Music producer, Berlin

Producer — Berlin

madein2k is a Berlin-based music producer known for his distinctive sound, signature “forever” producer tag and his work with some of the most influential artists in contemporary European rap. Closely connected to Luciano’s musical evolution, he has contributed to numerous releases and projects, with his productions generating over 900 million streams worldwide — and counting.

His credits include Luciano, La Mano 1.9, Sosa La M, Thizzy52, D-Block Europe, Shiva, Shindy, Pashanim, Jazeek and Kalim.

Beyond his production work, madein2k also releases his own music as an artist.
