About

madein2k is a Berlin-based producer behind some of contemporary European rap's biggest records — and the signature "forever" tag. His productions have crossed 900M+ streams, with credits from Luciano and La Mano 1.9 to D-Block Europe, Shindy, Pashanim, Jazeek and Kalim. He also releases his own music as an artist.
